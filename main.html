Who are we?

The Third Eye is the only Indian dance group in Helsingborg,Sweden. We perform classical, semi-classical, folk, fusion and other forms of Indian dance.

Our goal is to preserve the rich cultural heritage of the Indian dance forms but also keep room for innovation.

We have performed in Durga Puja 2017 , Helsingborg and Kali Puja 2017, Malm√∂. Our most recent performances were in Hx festival 2018, Helsingborg and Basant Mela 2018, Botkyrka Kommun, Stockholm.

We are getting ready to perform in Durga Puja, Helsingborg 2018 hosted by Bengali Cultural Society, South Sweden.

We hope to receive your encouragement and appreciation in our future endeavours.
